Visual Identity, Resturant

The logotype for Nyrén embodies the restaurant’s rustic and refined aesthetic. The design features a bold sans-serif “N” as the foundation, with “yrén” in smaller letters beside it for a balanced yet modern touch. A striking illustration of a pair of antlers extends to the right of the “N,” arching over “yrén,” reinforcing the brand’s connection to nature and rugged elegance.

A smaller version of the logotype has been designed for more compact branding needs. In this variation, all the letters are roughly the same size, creating a more balanced and streamlined look. The antlers are separated and placed below, framing the beginning and end of the name like a delicate wreath. This version maintains the rustic charm while offering a more versatile application for menus, packaging, and merchandise.

This visual identity complements the restaurant’s interior, which blends wood, exposed brick, black-painted metal, and Western-inspired details. The use of warm, earthy tones and natural textures across branding materials will ensure a strong, recognizable presence across signage, menus, and digital platforms.
