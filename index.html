Hi, I’m Wilma, a 19-year-old student of New Media Design at Jönköping University in Sweden. My program offers a dynamic mix of theoretical and practical learning, covering areas like graphic design, UX design, and programming.

I have a deep passion for graphic design and all things art and visual creativity. Recently in my courses, I’ve started building an exciting new skill: programming! Although it’s challenging, I find it both fun and rewarding.

My favorite creative outlet is working in Adobe Illustrator, especially designing logos. Outside of school, I enjoy painting with watercolors—it’s my way of blending relaxation with artistic expression.
